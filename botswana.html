Geography

Location

Southern Africa, north of South Africa

Terrain

The terrain is predominantly flat to general rolling tableland.

The Kalahari Desert is in the southwest.

Natural Resources

Natural resources include:

  • Diamonds
  • Copper
  • Nickel
  • Salt
  • Soda ash
  • Potash
  • Coal
  • Iron ore
  • Silver

Environment

Climate

The climate in Botswana is semiarid, with warm winters and hot summers.

Current Issues

  • Overgrazing
  • Desertification
  • Limited freshwater resources
  • Air pollution
