What is Photography

Photography means "Drawing with light"

It is the art of creating images by recording light through either a sensor or through film.

Why is Photography Important?

  1. Captures memories that are frozen in time
  2. Teaches us important details about the past
  3. A way to tell and preserve stories
  4. Helps people express themselves
  5. Photography is an inspirational art
